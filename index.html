🦋 Western Tiger Swallowtail ／ 西方虎鳳蝶

Papilio rutulus — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
These are large butterflies with a wingspan of 2¾ to 4 inches (7–10 cm), making them one of the largest butterflies in western gardens. They are predominantly yellow and black, with the wings striped like a tiger — four black stripes on yellow — on both the upper and lower surfaces. The hind-wings have elegant "tails" reminiscent of a swallow's tail feathers, which is how the family got its name. The lower inner margins of the hind-wings also display a beautiful dot of blue and orange. Females are larger than males but otherwise appear similar.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西方虎鳳蝶是體型相當大的蝴蝶，翼展可達 7 至 10 公分，是西部花園中最大型的蝴蝶之一。翅膀以鮮黃色為底，帶有四條黑色虎紋，無論正面或背面皆相同。後翅末端有優雅的「尾突」，形似燕子的尾羽，這也是「燕尾蝶（Swallowtail）」名稱的由來。後翅內緣還有美麗的藍色與橙色斑點。雌蝶體型略大於雄蝶，但外觀相似。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
These butterflies are found from the northwestern regions of Canada all the way down to the southern tip of Baja California, extending eastward through the Dakotas, Colorado, and New Mexico. They prefer woodlands near rivers and streams, but are also commonly found in wooded suburbs, parks, and roadsides. Whittier Narrows Nature Center, with its riparian corridors along the San Gabriel River, is prime habitat for this species!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西方虎鳳蝶的分布從加拿大西北部一路延伸至下加利福尼亞半島南端，向東涵蓋達科他州、科羅拉多州及新墨西哥州。牠們偏好棲息在河流與溪流旁的林地，也常見於樹木繁茂的郊區、公園與路邊。惠提爾納洛斯自然中心沿著聖蓋博河的河岸林帶，正是這種蝴蝶理想的棲息環境！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed on the leaves of riparian host plants including willows, cottonwoods, aspen, alder, elm, and California Sycamore.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲以河岸植物的葉片為食，常見寄主植物包括柳樹、棉白楊、白楊、赤楊、榆樹及加州懸鈴木。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult butterflies feed on flower nectar from thistles, California buckeye, zinnia, and many others, making them important pollinators. Males also gather at streams and mud puddles to drink minerals — a behavior called "mud-puddling."
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以薊花、加州七葉樹、百日草等花蜜為食，是重要的授粉者。雄蝶還常聚集在溪流或水窪旁「吸水」，這種行為稱為「泥濘吸水（mud-puddling）」，目的是從泥水中攝取礦物質。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
The Western Tiger Swallowtail undergoes complete metamorphosis: egg → caterpillar → chrysalis → adult.
  • Eggs: Females lay shiny green eggs one at a time on host plant leaves — up to 100 eggs total.
  • Caterpillars: Young caterpillars resemble bird droppings for camouflage. As they grow, they turn bright green with large yellow eyespots. They can also extend an orange forked organ called the osmeterium to mimic a snake and scare off predators.
  • Chrysalis: Summer chrysalids are bright green; fall/winter ones turn brown to blend with wood.
  • Adults: Adult lifespan is only 6 to 14 days.
  • Broods: In warm Southern California, there are 2–3 generations per year. Summer development can take as little as 15–20 days from egg to butterfly.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西方虎鳳蝶經歷完全變態：卵 → 幼蟲 → 蛹 → 成蝶。
  • 卵：雌蝶將亮綠色的卵一粒粒產在寄主植物葉片上，一生可產下多達一百粒。
  • 幼蟲：剛孵化時外觀像鳥糞，是保護色。成長後變為鮮綠色，帶有黃色大眼斑。遇到天敵時會伸出橙紅色的「臭角（osmeterium）」，模仿蛇的樣子嚇退敵人。
  • 蛹：夏季的蛹呈鮮綠色；秋冬季的蛹變成褐色，與枯木融為一體。
  • 成蝶：壽命只有短短 6 至 14 天。
  • 世代數：溫暖的南加州每年可繁殖 2 至 3 個世代，夏季從卵到羽化最快只需 15 至 20 天。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Males patrol canyons and hilltops near water, waiting for receptive females to mate. After fertilization, the female lays eggs one at a time on different host plants to ensure each caterpillar has enough food. The cycle then begins again.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
雄蝶在峽谷、山丘頂端或水塘附近來回巡邏飛行，守候雌蝶前來交配。雌蝶受精後，將卵一粒粒產在不同寄主植物的葉片上，確保每隻幼蟲都有充足食物，生命的循環就此重新開始。

🌍 Conservation Status ／ 保育現況

🇺🇸 English
The Western Tiger Swallowtail is rated G5 — Globally Secure, meaning its overall population is healthy and stable. No special conservation measures are currently required.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西方虎鳳蝶目前被評定為「全球穩定（G5）」等級，代表整體族群數量健康穩定，目前不需要特別的保育措施。

🦋 Pipevine Swallowtail ／ 管花鳳蝶

Battus philenor — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

💙 The "Blue Swallowtail" of California 加州「藍色鳳蝶」： The Pipevine Swallowtail is famous for its brilliant iridescent blue hindwings. By feeding on toxic pipevine plants, it makes itself poisonous at every life stage — from egg to adult. Other butterflies across North America even mimic its colors to borrow its protection!

管花鳳蝶以後翅上那片璀璨、閃耀著金屬光澤的藍色而聞名。藉由取食有毒的管花（馬兜鈴）植物，牠讓自己在每個生命階段——從卵到成蝶——都對天敵有毒。北美洲其他蝴蝶甚至模仿牠的外觀來借用牠的保護！

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Pipevine Swallowtail has a wingspan of 2¾ to 5 inches (7–13 cm). The forewings are solid black on top and grayish below. The real showstopper is the hindwing: the upper surface of the male's hindwing glows with a vivid, iridescent blue-green metallic sheen — one of the most beautiful wing colors in North American butterflies. Females have the same pattern but with a duller, less iridescent blue. On the underside of the hindwing, both sexes display a gorgeous row of seven bright orange spots embedded in the metallic blue. The California subspecies (B. p. hirsuta) is slightly smaller and noticeably hairier than populations from other parts of the US.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
管花鳳蝶翼展約 7 至 13 公分。前翅正面為純黑色，背面呈灰色。真正令人驚嘆的是後翅：雄蝶後翅正面呈現閃耀的藍綠色金屬光澤，是北美洲蝴蝶中最美麗的翅色之一。雌蝶花紋相同，但藍色光澤較暗淡。後翅背面，雌雄皆有一排七個鮮豔的橙色斑點鑲嵌在金屬藍中。加州亞種（B. p. hirsuta）體型略小，且比其他地區的族群明顯更為多毛。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
Found across much of the United States from New England south to Florida, west to Arizona, California, and Oregon, and south to Mexico. In California, it is considered the signature riparian butterfly — occurring along streams in foothill canyons wherever its host plant, California Pipevine (Aristolochia californica), grows. Its preferred habitats include open woodlands, woodland edges, streamsides, chaparral, meadows, and backyard gardens. Whittier Narrows, with its riparian corridors and open areas, is excellent habitat!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
管花鳳蝶分布廣泛，從美國東北部的新英格蘭地區向南至佛羅里達州，向西延伸至亞利桑那州、加州及奧勒岡州，並向南延伸至墨西哥。在加州，牠被認為是最具代表性的河岸帶蝴蝶——凡是有加州馬兜鈴（Aristolochia californica）生長的丘陵峽谷溪流旁，幾乎都能發現牠的蹤跡。牠偏好的棲地包括開闊林地、林緣地帶、溪流旁、灌木叢林、草地及庭院花園。惠提爾納洛斯是絕佳棲地！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars & The Toxic Secret
Caterpillars feed exclusively on plants of the genus Aristolochia — commonly known as pipevines or Dutchman's pipes. In California, the host plant is California Pipevine (Aristolochia californica). These plants contain powerful toxic compounds called aristolochic acids. The caterpillars ingest and store these toxins in their bodies, making themselves unpalatable and poisonous to predators. Remarkably, the female even passes these toxins into her eggs, so even the eggs are protected! Young caterpillars feed in groups, but become solitary as they grow.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲與毒素秘密
毛毛蟲專門以Aristolochia屬植物（通稱「馬兜鈴」）為食。在加州，寄主植物為加州馬兜鈴（Aristolochia californica）。這類植物含有強效毒素——馬兜鈴酸（aristolochic acids）。毛毛蟲攝食後將毒素儲存在體內，使自己對天敵來說既難吃又有毒。更令人驚嘆的是，雌蝶甚至將這些毒素傳入卵中，連卵都受到保護！幼齡毛毛蟲群體覓食，隨著成長逐漸轉為獨居。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult Pipevine Swallowtails feed on flower nectar from a wide variety of plants. Favorites include thistles, California buckeye, lupines, phlox, verbenas, lantana, and milkweed. Males are also frequently seen "mud-puddling" — gathering at moist soil to absorb minerals, which they also transfer to females during mating.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以各種植物的花蜜為食，最喜愛薊花、加州七葉樹、羽扇豆、天藍繡球、馬鞭草、馬纓丹及馬利筋等。雄蝶也常見「泥濘吸水（mud-puddling）」行為，並在交配時將礦物質轉移給雌蝶。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
The Pipevine Swallowtail undergoes complete metamorphosis: egg → caterpillar → chrysalis → adult.
  • Eggs: Females lay small, round, brick-red eggs in clusters of up to 20 on young shoot tips of the pipevine plant. Eggs are already toxic — protected by toxins from the mother.
  • Caterpillars: Dark reddish-black to black, covered with fleshy orange-red tubercles (spiky projections) running down its back. These bold warning colors signal to predators: "I am toxic — do not eat me!"
  • Chrysalis: Either brown or green with a delicate golden filigree pattern. Some pupae develop directly into adults within weeks; others enter diapause (dormancy) and overwinter.
  • Adults: In California, adults fly from late February or March through October. The number of broods can be one, two, three, or four per year depending on conditions.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
管花鳳蝶經歷完全變態：卵 → 幼蟲 → 蛹 → 成蝶。
  • 卵：雌蝶將小而圓、磚紅色的卵成簇產在馬兜鈴植株的嫩芽尖端，每簇多達 20 粒。卵已含有毒素，受到母蝶的保護。
  • 幼蟲：深紅黑色至黑色，背部排列著肉質的橙紅色突起（棘狀肉突）。鮮豔的警戒色向天敵宣告：「我有毒——請勿靠近！」
  • 蛹：呈褐色或綠色，帶有細緻的金色花紋。部分蛹在數週內直接羽化；另一些則進入滯育（diapause）休眠狀態，以蛹越冬。
  • 成蝶：在加州，成蝶飛行期從二月底或三月延續至十月。每年世代數因地點與氣候條件而異，可能是一代至四代。

🎭 Nature's Most Imitated Butterfly ／ 大自然模仿最多的蝴蝶

🇺🇸 English
The Pipevine Swallowtail's toxic reputation is so powerful that several other butterfly species have evolved to mimic its appearance — including the Spicebush Swallowtail, Black Swallowtail, and Red-Spotted Purple. Interestingly, in California there are currently no mimics of the Pipevine Swallowtail — because the species only arrived in California relatively recently in evolutionary terms, and there has not been enough time for other species to evolve mimicry here yet!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
管花鳳蝶有毒的名聲如此響亮，以至於北美洲有好幾種其他蝴蝶演化出了模擬牠外觀的能力，包括鱷梨鳳蝶、黑鳳蝶及紅斑紫蛺蝶。有趣的是，加州目前沒有任何蝴蝶模仿管花鳳蝶——因為這種蝴蝶在演化時間尺度上是相當晚近才到達加州的，其他物種還沒有足夠的時間在這裡演化出模仿行為！
💙

「這種蝴蝶在生命的每一個階段都有毒——連卵也不例外！母蝶將植物毒素直接傳入卵中，在卵孵化之前就提供保護。」

🦋 Anise Swallowtail ／ 茴芹鳳蝶

Papilio zelicaon — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

🌿 Urban Survivor 城市倖存者： The Anise Swallowtail has brilliantly adapted to human-altered landscapes. Today it thrives in gardens, vacant lots, and roadsides by using introduced fennel and poison hemlock instead of its original native host plants. It is one of the most commonly seen swallowtails in California cities!

茴芹鳳蝶出色地適應了人類改變的環境。如今牠以引進的茴香與毒芹取代原有的本土寄主植物，活躍於花園、空地及路邊，是加州城市中最常見的鳳蝶之一！

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Anise Swallowtail has a wingspan of 2 to 3.1 inches (52–80 mm). Its wings are mostly yellow with broad black bands along the edges. A key identification point: the leading edge of the forewing is almost entirely solid black, giving this species a distinctly "blacker" appearance compared to the Western Tiger Swallowtail. There are striking blue spots on the rear edge of the hindwing, and yellowish-orange to red eyespots with black pupils near the tails. There is no obvious difference between males and females.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
茴芹鳳蝶翼展約 52 至 80 公釐。翅膀以黃色為主，翅緣有寬黑色帶。重要的辨識特點：前翅前緣幾乎全黑，使這個物種的外觀比西方虎鳳蝶明顯更黑。後翅後緣有醒目的藍色斑點，尾部附近有黃橙至紅色眼斑，帶有黑色瞳孔。雌雄外觀相似，沒有明顯的差異。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
Found throughout western North America from British Columbia south to Baja California and east to the Rocky Mountains. This species is extremely adaptable — found from sea level all the way to 14,000 feet elevation. In California, it is commonly seen in gardens, vacant lots, fields, roadsides, and urban areas wherever fennel grows. The open riparian areas of Whittier Narrows are perfect habitat.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
分布於北美西部，從英屬哥倫比亞向南至下加利福尼亞半島，向東至落磯山脈。這個物種的適應力極強——從海平面到海拔 4,400 公尺皆有分布。在加州，凡是有茴香生長的花園、空地、田野及路邊都能見到。惠提爾納洛斯的開闊河岸地帶是絕佳棲地。

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed on plants in the carrot family (Apiaceae). In California coastal areas, they primarily feed on sweet fennel (anise) and poison hemlock — both introduced European weeds. At higher elevations they use native plants like lomatium and other native parsleys. When threatened, the caterpillar can extend a foul-smelling orange forked organ called the osmeterium from behind its head — it mimics a snake's tongue and smells terrible to predators!
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲以傘形科（Apiaceae）植物為食。在加州沿海地區，主要以甜茴香（茴芹）及毒芹為食——兩者都是引進的歐洲雜草。在較高海拔地區，則以本土鐔芹等原生植物為食。受到威脅時，毛毛蟲可以從頭部後方伸出散發惡臭的橙色分叉「臭角（osmeterium）」——模仿蛇的舌頭，令天敵退縮！
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult Anise Swallowtails feed on nectar from a wide variety of flowers. They are particularly fond of thistles, milkweed, and various native wildflowers. They are important pollinators in both natural and urban landscapes throughout California.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以各種植物的花蜜為食，尤其喜愛薊花、馬利筋及各種本土野花。在自然與城市環境中都是重要的授粉者。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis; egg to adult in 30–60 days.
  • Eggs: Small, round, yellow, laid singly on host plant leaves or stems.
  • Caterpillars: Young caterpillars start black and white, mimicking bird droppings for camouflage. As they grow through multiple molts, they become bright green with black bands and orange/yellow spots — striking and beautiful. When alarmed, they extend the orange osmeterium, which releases a foul butyric acid smell.
  • Chrysalis: Looks remarkably like a small thick branch — brown or green, held firmly to the plant by silk. In northern areas, the chrysalis overwinters.
  • Adults: In Southern California, several generations fly from late February through October.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
完全變態，卵到成蝶約 30 至 60 天。
  • 卵：小而圓、黃色，單粒產於寄主植物的葉片或莖上。
  • 幼蟲：初期為黑白色，擬態鳥糞保護自己。隨著多次蛻皮成長，變為鮮綠色帶黑色條紋及橙黃色斑點——令人印象深刻。受到驚嚇時，會伸出橙色臭角，釋放出一種類似腐敗奶油的惡臭。
  • 蛹：外觀酷似一截小樹枝——褐色或綠色，以絲線牢固地固定在植物上。北部地區的蛹以蛹態越冬。
  • 成蝶：在南加州，從二月下旬至十月有數個世代。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Males practice "hilltopping" — flying to the tops of bare hills or prominent high points to establish territories and wait for females. Both sexes use chemical pheromones detected through receptors on their legs, feet, and antennae to find suitable mates. After mating, females lay eggs singly on host plant leaves, and the cycle begins again.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
雄蝶有「登頂（hilltopping）」行為——飛到光禿的山丘頂或高點建立領域等候雌蝶。雌雄均透過腳、足及觸角上的化學感受器偵測費洛蒙尋找配偶。交配後，雌蝶將卵一粒粒產在寄主植物葉片上，生命的循環再次展開。

🦋 Western Giant Swallowtail ／ 西方巨鳳蝶

Papilio rumiko — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

👑 North America's Largest Butterfly 北美最大蝴蝶： The Western Giant Swallowtail is one of the largest butterflies in North America, with a wingspan up to 6.25 inches! It was only recognized as a separate species from the Eastern Giant Swallowtail in 2014 based on DNA evidence. It is commonly seen in Southern California gardens — especially near citrus trees.

西方巨鳳蝶是北美洲最大的蝴蝶之一，翼展可達約 16 公分！直到 2014 年才根據 DNA 證據從東方巨鳳蝶中獨立出來成為單獨物種。在南加州的庭院中常見——尤其是在柑橘樹附近。

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Western Giant Swallowtail is truly enormous — wingspan 4 to 6.25 inches (10–16 cm), making it immediately recognizable in flight. Its wings are dark brown to black with two bold yellow bands: one horizontal across the forewing and one diagonal across the hindwing, forming a large yellow "X" pattern. The underside of the wings is predominantly yellow with black accents — quite different from the dark upper surface. The hindwings have characteristic swallowtail tails. The Western Giant is slightly smaller with paler yellow than its Eastern cousin.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西方巨鳳蝶真的非常巨大——翼展 10 至 16 公分，飛行時一眼就能辨認。翅膀為深棕至黑色，帶有兩條醒目的黃色帶：前翅一條橫帶，後翅一條斜帶——形成大型黃色「X」圖案。翅膀背面以黃色為主帶有黑色裝飾——與深色的正面形成鮮明對比。後翅有典型的鳳蝶尾突。西方種比東方種略小，黃色較淡。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
Found from California east to Texas, south through Mexico to Panama. In California it is common in the southern part of the state, particularly in the Los Angeles area. This butterfly favors open woodlands and woodland edges close to the ground, and is especially attracted to gardens with citrus trees. Neighborhoods around Whittier Narrows with citrus trees are excellent places to spot it!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
分布從加州向東至德克薩斯州，向南穿越墨西哥至巴拿馬。在加州主要見於南部地區，尤其是洛杉磯一帶。這種蝴蝶偏好靠近地面的開闊林地及林緣地帶，對柑橘樹特別有吸引力。惠提爾納洛斯周邊種有柑橘樹的住宅區是發現牠的絕佳地點！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars ("Orange Dogs")
Caterpillars feed on plants in the rue family (Rutaceae) — primarily citrus trees such as orange, lemon, lime, and grapefruit. The caterpillar is nicknamed the "orange dog" because of its habit of feeding on citrus. It is one of the most convincing bird-dropping mimics in the insect world — large, brown, cream, and white with a saddle-shaped pattern that makes it almost indistinguishable from real bird droppings. Even large caterpillars 2 inches long use this camouflage successfully!
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲（「橙狗」）

🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以馬利筋、馬纓丹、醉魚草、百日草等花蜜為食。飛行能力強，速度快，吸蜜時翅膀持續拍動——給攝影帶來不小的挑戰！

🦋 California Dogface Butterfly ／ 加州狗臉蝶

Zerene eurydice — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

⭐ California's Official State Insect 加州州蟲： The California Dogface Butterfly holds a very special distinction — it is California's official State Insect, designated in 1972, making California the first state in the nation to adopt an official state insect! It is found nowhere else on Earth except California and a small part of Baja California — making it a true symbol of California's unique natural heritage.

加州狗臉蝶擁有一個非常特殊的頭銜——牠是加州的官方州蟲（State Insect），於1972年正式指定，使加州成為全美第一個設立州蟲的州！除了加州及下加利福尼亞半島的一小部分外，地球上任何其他地方都找不到牠。

🐩 Appearance — The "Poodle Face" ／ 外觀特徵——「貴賓狗的臉」

🇺🇸 English
The California Dogface has a wingspan of about 2 to 2½ inches (5.1–6.3 cm). The name comes from the striking pattern on the male's wings: the black border on the forewing outlines a shape that looks remarkably like a poodle's head — you can make out the ears, eye (a black dot), and nose in the yellow-orange wing. The male also has an iridescent, almost metallic sheen on the forewings that shimmers in sunlight — caused by light-reflecting structures in the wing scales. The female is much plainer — mostly pale yellow or cream-colored with just one or two small black spots on the forewing. She lacks the dramatic poodle pattern entirely.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
加州狗臉蝶翼展約 5.1 至 6.3 公分。「狗臉蝶」的名稱來自雄蝶翅膀上的獨特圖案：前翅的黑色邊框圍出了一個酷似貴賓狗頭部的輪廓——您可以在橙黃色的翅膀上辨認出狗耳、眼睛（一個黑點）和鼻子。雄蝶前翅還有一種彩虹般的金屬光澤，在陽光下閃閃發光——這是由翅鱗中的反光幾丁質結構所產生的。雌蝶的外觀則樸素許多，主體為淡黃色或乳白色，前翅僅有一至兩個小黑點，完全沒有雄蝶那般戲劇性的貴賓狗圖案。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
The California Dogface is endemic to California — meaning it exists nowhere else in the world except California and the very northern tip of Baja California. Its range runs from north-central California southward, between the Sierra Nevada and the Coast Ranges. It is most commonly found in the Santa Ana Mountains and San Bernardino Mountains in Southern California — right in our backyard! Its preferred habitats are foothills, chaparral, and oak or coniferous woodlands — especially near riparian areas where its host plant, False Indigo, grows. Despite being the state insect, it is considered rare and local — most Californians have never seen one in the wild!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
加州狗臉蝶是加州特有種——除了加州及下加利福尼亞半島最北端，全球任何地方都找不到牠。分布範圍從加州中北部向南延伸，位於內華達山脈與海岸山脈之間。在南加州，最常見於聖塔安那山脈（Santa Ana Mountains）與聖貝納迪諾山脈（San Bernardino Mountains）——正好就在我們附近！牠偏好的棲地為丘陵地、灌木叢林（chaparral）及橡樹林或針葉林，尤其是靠近溪流的河岸地帶，因為那裡生長著牠賴以為生的假靛藍（False Indigo）。雖貴為加州州蟲，牠卻被認為是稀少且分布局限的物種——大多數加州人從未在野外親眼見過牠！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
The caterpillar feeds exclusively on one plant: California False Indigo (Amorpha californica). This is the only known host plant, making this butterfly's survival completely dependent on the health of this single shrub species. The caterpillar is bright green with pale horizontal stripes — perfectly camouflaged against the leaves of the False Indigo. The eggs also start green or yellow-green, then turn a vivid crimson as they mature — a remarkable detail to share with visitors!
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲只吃一種植物：加州假靛藍（Amorpha californica）。這是目前已知唯一的寄主植物，意味著這種蝴蝶的生存完全取決於這一種灌木的健康狀況。毛毛蟲為鮮綠色，帶有淺色橫紋，與假靛藍的葉片完美融合。卵初為綠色或黃綠色，隨著成熟逐漸轉為深紅色——這是可以與訪客分享的另一個精彩細節！
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult Dogface butterflies feed on flower nectar, showing a strong preference for purple flowers. Favorite nectar plants include thistles, tall blue verbena, and California buckeye. Their fast flight — typically 10 to 20 feet above the ground — makes them very hard to observe. Your best chance to spot one is when it pauses to feed at a flower!
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以花蜜為食，對紫色花朵情有獨鍾。最喜愛的蜜源植物包括薊花、高大藍馬鞭草（tall blue verbena）及加州七葉樹等。由於飛行速度極快，平時飛翔於離地 3 至 6 公尺的高空，最佳的觀察機會是在牠停在花朵上吸食花蜜的時候！

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis. Two broods per year:
  • Eggs: Laid one at a time on False Indigo leaves. Start pale green or yellow, then turn a striking crimson color as they develop.
  • Caterpillars: Bright greenish-yellow with white lateral bands edged in red — matching the color of their host plant for camouflage. Pass through several instars before pupating.
  • Chrysalis: Bright green, blending in with the False Indigo foliage. Takes approximately one week to complete metamorphosis in summer.
  • First brood: Flies April to July, peaking in June. Adult lifespan: about 3 to 4 months.
  • Second brood: Flies August to October. Lifespan: 6–8 months — these adults overwinter in a hibernation-like state and emerge in early spring to mate.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
完全變態。每年兩個世代：
  • 卵：一粒粒產在假靛藍的葉片上。初為淡綠色或黃色，隨後轉變為醒目的深紅色。
  • 幼蟲：鮮豔的黃綠色，側面有白色縱帶，邊緣鑲有紅色——與假靛藍葉片顏色相近，提供保護色。經歷數次蛻皮後化蛹。
  • 蛹：鮮綠色，與假靛藍的葉叢融為一體。夏季的蛹約需一週完成蛻變。
  • 第一代：飛行期為四月至七月，六月達到高峰。成蝶壽命約 3 至 4 個月。
  • 第二代：飛行期為八月至十月。壽命 6–8 個月——牠們會以類似冬眠的狀態越冬，翌年早春再度出現，交配後展開新的生命循環。

🦋 Red Admiral Butterfly ／ 紅蛺蝶

Vanessa atalanta — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Red Admiral is a medium-sized butterfly with a wingspan of 1¾ to 3 inches (4.5–7.6 cm). Its wings are striking black with bold orange-red diagonal bands across the forewings and along the outer edge of the hindwings. The forewings also display a cluster of white spots near the tips. The underside of the wings is beautifully mottled in brown, black, and blue — excellent camouflage when the wings are closed.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
紅蛺蝶是中型蝴蝶，翼展約 4.5 至 7.6 公分。翅膀底色為黑色，前翅上有醒目的橙紅色斜帶，後翅外緣也有橙紅色條紋，前翅尖端還有一簇白色斑點。翅膀合攏時，翅膀內側呈現棕色、黑色與藍色交錯的花紋，是絕佳的保護色。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
The Red Admiral is one of the most widely distributed butterflies in the world, found across North and Central America, Europe, North Africa, and Asia. In North America, its range extends from Guatemala north through Mexico and the United States all the way to northern Canada, and also includes Hawaii and some Caribbean islands. It thrives in moist woods, parks, yards, marshes, and moist fields. Here in the Los Angeles area, Whittier Narrows — with its moist riparian habitat — is a perfect spot to find them!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
紅蛺蝶是世界上分布最廣的蝴蝶之一，遍布北美洲、中美洲、歐洲、北非及亞洲。在北美洲，分布範圍從瓜地馬拉向北經過墨西哥、美國一直延伸到加拿大北部，夏威夷與加勒比海部分島嶼也有其蹤跡。牠們偏好潮濕的林地、公園、庭院、沼澤及濕潤的草地。惠提爾納洛斯潮濕的河岸環境，正是欣賞紅蛺蝶的絕佳地點！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed exclusively on plants in the nettle family (Urticaceae). This includes stinging nettle, wood nettle, false nettle, and pellitory. In California, Common Nettle (Urtica dioica) and Pennsylvania Pellitory are the confirmed host plants. The Red Admiral's ability to use over 50 nettle-family plant species worldwide is one reason it is so widespread and adaptable.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲專門以蕁麻科（Urticaceae）植物為食，包括刺蕁麻、木蕁麻、假蕁麻及牆草等。在加州，確認的寄主植物為普通蕁麻（Urtica dioica）與賓州牆草。紅蛺蝶能利用全球超過五十種蕁麻科植物，這正是牠能廣泛分布、適應力極強的原因之一。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult Red Admirals have surprisingly diverse tastes! They actually prefer tree sap flows, fermenting fruit, and even bird droppings over flower nectar. When those aren't available, they visit flowers such as red clover, aster, and butterfly bush. Like many butterflies, they also "wick" from mud puddles to absorb minerals and electrolytes.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶的口味出乎意料地多元！牠們其實偏好樹木滲出的汁液、發酵腐爛的水果，甚至是鳥糞，花蜜反而是次要選擇。當這些食物來源不足時，牠們才會訪花，包括紅花苜蓿、紫苑花及醉魚草等。牠們也會在泥水窪旁「吸水」，攝取礦物質與電解質。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
The Red Admiral undergoes complete metamorphosis: egg → caterpillar → chrysalis → adult.
  • Eggs: Females lay eggs singly on the tops of host plant leaves. Eggs are green to cream in color and covered in tiny hair-like structures that help them blend in with nettle leaves.
  • Caterpillars: Larvae grow to about 1¼ inches (3.5 cm). They are typically black with white spots and spines. Young caterpillars fold leaves into shelters to hide; older caterpillars build nests of leaves tied together with silk.
  • Chrysalis: The hanging chrysalis brilliantly mimics a dry, dead leaf in both color and texture.
  • Adults: Adults can hibernate through winter and re-emerge in spring with a lifespan that can extend several months when overwintering.
  • Broods: In California: 2–3 broods per year, with flight season from March through November.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
紅蛺蝶經歷完全變態：卵 → 幼蟲 → 蛹 → 成蝶。
  • 卵：雌蝶將卵一粒粒產在寄主植物葉片的正面。卵呈綠色至乳白色，表面覆有細小的毛狀結構，有助於與蕁麻葉片融為一體。
  • 幼蟲：毛毛蟲體長可達約 3.5 公分，通常為黑色帶白色斑點與棘刺。幼齡毛毛蟲會將葉片折疊成「葉巢」躲藏其中；較大的毛毛蟲則用絲線將葉片綁在一起築巢。
  • 蛹：垂懸的蛹在顏色與質感上完美模仿一片乾枯的枯葉。
  • 成蝶：成蝶可以越冬休眠，春天再重新出現。越冬的個體壽命可長達數個月。
  • 世代數：在加州：每年 2 至 3 個世代，飛行季節從三月延續至十一月。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Male Red Admirals are highly territorial. Each male claims an elliptical area roughly 4–13 meters wide and patrols its boundaries up to thirty times per hour! They perch on sunlit spots in the mid-afternoon, watching for females to fly past. Despite this aggression toward rivals, Red Admirals are famously people-friendly and will often land on humans, using them as a perch!

🦋 Monarch Butterfly ／ 帝王蝶

Danaus plexippus — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

⚠️ Conservation Alert 保育警訊： In December 2024, the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service proposed listing the Monarch as a threatened species under the Endangered Species Act. Western populations have declined over 90% since the 1980s. This butterfly needs our help!

2024年12月，美國魚類及野生動物管理局提議將帝王蝶列為《瀕危物種法》下的「受威脅物種」。西部族群自1980年代以來已減少逾90%。這種美麗的蝴蝶，迫切需要我們的關注與保護！

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Monarch is one of North America's most recognizable butterflies. Its wingspan averages 3.5 to 4 inches (90–100 mm), making it a large and conspicuous species. The wings are a bold, bright orange with black borders and black veins, accented by two rows of white spots along the outer edges. Males and females look similar, but males have a small black scent patch on each hind wing, while females have thicker black veining. On average, an adult Monarch weighs less than half a gram — lighter than a paperclip!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
帝王蝶是北美洲辨識度最高的蝴蝶之一，翼展平均約 90 至 100 公釐，是體型醒目的大型蝶種。翅膀呈鮮豔的橙色，帶有黑色邊框與黑色翅脈，翅緣還有兩排白色斑點。雌雄外觀相似，但雄蝶後翅各有一個小型黑色氣味腺斑；雌蝶的翅脈則較雄蝶粗黑。一隻成蝶的體重平均不到半公克——比一枚迴紋針還輕！

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
Monarchs are native to North and South America but have spread worldwide to Australia, New Zealand, Hawaii, and parts of Europe. In North America, there are two major migratory populations: the Eastern population (east of the Rockies), which overwinters in the oyamel fir forests of central Mexico, and the Western population (west of the Rockies), which overwinters along the California coast — including famous sites like Pacific Grove and Pismo Beach. Breeding habitat requires the presence of milkweed plants. In western North America, milkweed and nectar resources are often found in riparian corridors — exactly the kind of habitat found at Whittier Narrows!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
帝王蝶原產於南北美洲，後來也擴散至澳洲、紐西蘭、夏威夷及歐洲部分地區。在北美洲，主要有兩大遷徙族群：東部族群（落磯山脈以東）越冬於墨西哥中部的冷杉林；西部族群（落磯山脈以西）越冬於加州沿岸，包括太平洋叢林市（Pacific Grove）及皮斯莫海灘（Pismo Beach）等著名越冬地。繁殖棲地必須有馬利筋（乳草）植物存在。在北美西部，馬利筋和蜜源植物常見於河岸廊道——這正是惠提爾納洛斯的典型棲息環境！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Monarch caterpillars feed exclusively on milkweed plants (genus Asclepias). Milkweed contains toxic cardiac glycosides, which the caterpillars store in their bodies. This makes both the caterpillar and the adult butterfly poisonous and distasteful to predators such as birds, frogs, and lizards. The bold orange-and-black coloring of the adult is a warning signal to predators: "Do not eat me!"
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
帝王蝶的毛毛蟲只吃馬利筋屬（Asclepias）植物。馬利筋含有毒性強烈的強心配糖體，毛毛蟲攝食後將毒素儲存在體內，使毛毛蟲與成蝶都對鳥類、青蛙、蜥蜴等天敵有毒且難以下嚥。成蝶鮮豔的橙黑色正是對天敵發出的警告信號：「我有毒，請勿靠近！」
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult Monarchs feed on nectar from a wide variety of flowers, especially native wildflowers such as goldenrod, aster, coneflower, and lantana. Nectar not only fuels their daily activity but also provides the fat reserves they need for their extraordinary long-distance migration and to survive overwintering.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以各種花卉的花蜜為食，尤其喜愛本土野花如一枝黃花、紫苑、松果菊及馬纓丹等。花蜜不僅提供日常活動所需的能量，更讓牠們儲積足夠的脂肪，以備漫長的遷徙旅途及越冬之用。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Monarchs undergo complete metamorphosis: egg → caterpillar → chrysalis → adult. The full cycle from egg to adult takes about 4 to 5 weeks.
  • Eggs: Females lay eggs singly on milkweed leaves. Eggs hatch in 3 to 5 days.
  • Caterpillars: The caterpillar feeds on milkweed for about 2 weeks, growing through 5 molts. Its bold yellow, black, and white stripes warn predators of its toxicity.
  • Chrysalis: The caterpillar forms a beautiful jade-green chrysalis with gold dots, spending 10 to 12 days transforming inside. As it nears emergence, the chrysalis becomes transparent, revealing the orange wings within.
  • Adults — Summer generations: Most adult Monarchs live only 2 to 5 weeks and focus on reproduction.
  • Adults — Migratory generation: The final generation of the year enters a special state called diapause — they stop reproducing and prepare for migration. This "super generation" can live 6 to 8 months, surviving the entire migration and overwintering period before mating in the spring.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
帝王蝶經歷完全變態：卵 → 幼蟲 → 蛹 → 成蝶。從卵到成蝶約需 4 至 5 週。
  • 卵：雌蝶將卵一粒粒產在馬利筋葉片上，3 至 5 天後孵化。
  • 幼蟲：毛毛蟲以馬利筋為食約兩週，經歷五次蛻皮成長。鮮豔的黃、黑、白相間條紋是對天敵的警示色。
  • 蛹：毛毛蟲化為美麗的翠綠色蛹，上有金色斑點，在蛹內蛻變約需 10 至 12 天。接近羽化時，蛹殼逐漸透明，可隱約看見其中橙色的翅膀。
  • 成蝶（夏季世代）：大多數成蝶壽命僅 2 至 5 週，主要任務是繁殖後代。
  • 成蝶（遷徙世代）：每年最後一個世代進入特殊的「滯育（diapause）」狀態——停止繁殖，全力準備遷徙。這一代「超級蝴蝶」壽命長達 6 至 8 個月，完成全程遷徙與越冬後，才在春天交配繁殖。

✈️ The Great Migration ／ 偉大的遷徙

🇺🇸 English
The Monarch's annual migration is one of the most spectacular natural phenomena in the world. Every autumn, millions of Monarchs begin their journey south. The eastern population travels up to 3,000 miles to the oyamel fir forests of central Mexico. The western population migrates to overwintering sites along the California coast. Monarchs can travel 50 to 100 miles per day, and the record for a single day is 265 miles! During migration, they use thermal air currents to glide and save energy, navigating with the sun as a compass combined with an internal biological clock. Remarkably, no single butterfly makes the full round trip — it takes 3 to 4 generations to complete the northward journey in spring and summer, but the single migratory "super generation" makes the entire southward journey in autumn alone.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
帝王蝶每年的遷徙，是世界上最壯觀的自然奇景之一。每到秋天，數以百萬計的帝王蝶展開南遷之旅。東部族群飛行多達 4,800 公里，抵達墨西哥中部的冷杉林；西部族群則遷往加州沿岸的越冬地。帝王蝶每天可飛行 80 至 160 公里，最高紀錄一天飛行了 426 公里！遷徙時，牠們借助熱氣流滑翔以節省體力，並以太陽作為指南針、配合體內的生物時鐘來導航。最令人驚嘆的是：沒有任何一隻蝴蝶能完成完整的來回旅程——春夏北返需要 3 至 4 個世代接力完成，但秋季南下那段漫長的旅程，卻由單獨一代「超級世代」獨自完成。

🦋 Mourning Cloak ／ 哀蛺蝶

Nymphalis antiopa — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

⏳ Champion of Longevity 壽命冠軍： The Mourning Cloak holds a remarkable record — it is one of the longest-lived butterflies in North America, with adults surviving 10 to 11 months! It hibernates through winter and is often the very first butterfly to appear in spring, sometimes flying while snow is still on the ground.

哀蛺蝶保有一項驚人紀錄——牠是北美洲壽命最長的蝴蝶之一，成蝶可存活 10 至 11 個月！牠冬眠越冬，常常是春天最早出現的蝴蝶，有時在積雪尚未融化時就已經飛翔。

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Mourning Cloak has a wingspan of 2.25 to 4 inches (6–10 cm). It is one of the most distinctive butterflies — no other species looks quite like it. The upper wing surface is a rich velvety dark maroon or brown, with a row of iridescent blue spots lining the inner edge of a bold pale-yellow (cream) border. The wing edges are ragged and scalloped. The underside is dark gray-brown with bark-like gray striation — excellent camouflage when resting on a tree. There is no obvious difference between males and females.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
哀蛺蝶翼展 6 至 10 公分。外觀最獨特的蝴蝶之一——沒有其他物種長得像牠。翅膀正面為濃郁的絲絨深栗色或褐色，內緣有一排彩虹色藍色斑點，外緣有醒目的淡黃色（乳白色）邊框。翅緣呈鋸齒狀。翅膀背面為深灰褐色，帶有樹皮狀紋路——停棲在樹上時是絕佳保護色。雌雄外觀相似。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
One of the most widely distributed butterflies in the Northern Hemisphere — found across all of North America south of the tundra to central Mexico, and across temperate Eurasia. Because they roam and migrate, they are found in almost any habitat where host plants occur: woods, parks, suburbs, and especially riparian areas. Whittier Narrows' willows and cottonwoods make it ideal Mourning Cloak habitat!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
北半球分布最廣的蝴蝶之一——遍布整個北美洲（從凍原以南至墨西哥中部）及溫帶歐亞大陸。由於牠們到處漫遊和遷徙，幾乎任何有寄主植物的棲地都能發現：林地、公園、郊區，尤其是河岸地帶。惠提爾納洛斯的柳樹和棉白楊是哀蛺蝶的理想棲地！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars, also known as "Spiny Elm Caterpillars," feed on willows, cottonwoods, aspens, elms, and birches. Young caterpillars feed gregariously in large communal webs — sometimes so many that they can completely defoliate branches. They are black with white spots and rows of red-orange dots, with long black spines covering the body.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲又稱「刺榆毛蟲（Spiny Elm Caterpillar）」，以柳樹、棉白楊、白楊、榆樹及樺樹為食。幼齡毛毛蟲群體在大型絲質巢網中覓食——有時數量眾多，足以將樹枝完全吃禿。牠們為黑色帶白斑及橙紅色點，全身帶有長黑刺。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Unusual among butterflies — Mourning Cloaks rarely visit flowers for nectar! They primarily feed on tree sap flows, rotting fruit, and fermenting fluids. They are often seen at tree wounds oozing sap. When flowers are the only available option, they will visit willows and other spring bloomers.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
在蝴蝶中較為特殊——哀蛺蝶很少訪花吸蜜！牠們主要以樹木滲出的汁液、腐爛水果及發酵液體為食，常見於樹木滲汁的傷口處。只有在沒有其他選擇時，牠們才會訪花。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis; total lifespan 10–11 months — one of the longest of any butterfly.
  • Eggs: Females lay eggs in clusters, arranged in rings around twigs of host trees. Eggs hatch in 10 to 12 days.
  • Caterpillars: Feed communally in webs for several weeks, then wander to find a pupation site.
  • Chrysalis: Tan to gray in color, up to 28 mm long, with two head horns. Hangs upside down from stems.
  • Adults: Emerge in summer → feed to build fat reserves → enter estivation (summer dormancy) → re-emerge in autumn → feed again → hibernate through winter → emerge in spring to mate and lay eggs.
  • Broods: 1–2 broods per year in California.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
完全變態；總壽命 10–11 個月——是所有蝴蝶中壽命最長之一。
  • 卵：雌蝶將卵成批產在寄主樹嫩枝上，排列成環狀圈。卵在 10 至 12 天孵化。
  • 幼蟲：在巢網中群體覓食數週，然後分散尋找化蛹地點。
  • 蛹：棕褐至灰色，長達 28 公釐，帶有兩個頭角。倒懸在莖桿上。
  • 成蝶：夏天羽化 → 覓食儲積脂肪 → 進入夏眠（夏季休眠）→ 秋天再度出現覓食 → 越冬冬眠 → 春天醒來交配產卵。
  • 世代數：加州每年 1 至 2 個世代。

🇺🇸 English
Males are territorial, defending sunny perches near ravines, wood margins, parks, and stream edges — sometimes territories over 300 square meters! The Painted Lady has a wingspan of 2 to 3 inches (50–76 mm). Its wings are a beautiful mosaic of orange, black, and white. The forewing tips are black with white splotches. 🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

Found on every continent except Antarctica. In North America, the entire population winters near the US-Mexico border and in the desert Southwest, then migrates northward each spring in waves. In some years — especially after heavy winter rains — billions make the journey north through California. They prefer open sunny habitats: fields, meadows, gardens, roadsides, and chaparral. Whittier Narrows is a key stopover point during California migrations!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
遍布除南極洲外所有大洲。在北美洲，整個族群在美墨邊境及沙漠西南部越冬，每年春天分波向北遷徙。在部分年份——尤其是冬雨豐沛之後——數十億隻穿越加州向北。偏好開闊陽光環境：田野、草地、花園、路邊及灌木叢林。惠提爾納洛斯是加州遷徙途中的重要中途站！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars are generalist feeders — they use over 100 recorded host plant species! Main hosts include thistles, mallows, hollyhock, lupine, fiddleneck, and cheeseweed. Each caterpillar builds a messy silken tent on the plant where it feeds and hides. Caterpillars are grayish-brown with black spiny bristles and a yellow stripe running along the back.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲為廣食性——已記錄的寄主植物超過100種！主要寄主包括薊、錦葵、蜀葵、羽扇豆、費德勒草、芝士草等。每隻毛毛蟲在植物上織出凌亂的絲質巢帳，在其中覓食和躲藏。毛毛蟲呈灰褐色，帶黑色棘刺及黃色背縱紋。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adults are also generalist nectar feeders — visiting over 100 plant species. They are especially fond of asters, thistles, cosmos, red clover, milkweed, ironweed, and Joe-Pye weed. Painted Ladies are important pollinators across many ecosystems worldwide.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶同樣是廣食性蜜源訪花者——訪問超過 100 種植物。尤其喜愛紫苑、薊花、秋英、紅花苜蓿、馬利筋及澤蘭等。是全球多種生態系統中重要的授粉者。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis; egg to adult in 33–44 days in warm weather.
  • Eggs: Small, pale green, barrel-shaped with vertical ribs, laid singly on host plant leaves.
  • Caterpillars: Go through 5 instars (molts) over about 2 weeks. Each caterpillar lives in a messy silken tent full of frass (droppings) — not the tidiest housekeeping!
  • Chrysalis: Metallic green, brown, or bluish-white with gold markings — quite beautiful.
  • Adults: Lifespan about 2 to 4 weeks. Multiple generations per year.

Males are territorial, perching in the late afternoon in sunny spots where females are likely to pass, often sharing sites with Red Admirals and West Coast Ladies. 🦋 Cabbage White Butterfly ／ 菜粉蝶

Pieris rapae — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

📌 Docent Note 導覽員小提醒： The Cabbage White is one of the most common butterflies you will ever see — yet most people walk right past it! Originally from Europe and Asia, it arrived in North America in the 1860s and has since spread to every corner of the continent. It is often the very first butterfly to appear each spring.

菜粉蝶是您最常見到的蝴蝶之一——但大多數人對它視而不見！牠原產於歐洲與亞洲，於1860年代抵達北美洲後，迅速遍布整個大陸。牠通常是每年春天最早現身的蝴蝶。

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Cabbage White is a small-to-medium butterfly with a wingspan of about 1.5 to 2.5 inches (4.5–6.5 cm). Its wings are creamy white with black-tipped forewings. Here is the easiest way to tell male from female: males have one black spot on each forewing, while females have two black spots. Both sexes have a single black spot on the upper hindwing, and the undersides of the wings have a soft yellowish tinge. The overall appearance is deceptively simple — pure white in the garden is this butterfly's signature look.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
菜粉蝶是中小型蝴蝶，翼展約 4.5 至 6.5 公分。翅膀為乳白色，前翅尖端帶有黑色。分辨雌雄最簡單的方法：雄蝶每片前翅有一個黑點，雌蝶則有兩個黑點。雌雄後翅正面各有一個黑點，翅膀背面則帶有淡淡的黃色調。外觀看似樸素，但那一抹純白正是牠在花園中最鮮明的標誌。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
The Cabbage White is one of the most widespread butterflies on Earth. It is native to Europe and Asia, originating in the Eastern Mediterranean region. It was accidentally introduced to Montreal, Canada around 1860 and has since spread across all of North America, as well as Australia and New Zealand. Today it is found on every continent except Antarctica. Its habitat is extremely versatile — it thrives in gardens, parks, roadsides, weedy fields, farms, and urban areas. Wherever there are plants in the mustard or cabbage family, you will likely find this butterfly.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
菜粉蝶是地球上分布最廣的蝴蝶之一，原產於歐洲與亞洲，起源於地中海東部地區。約於1860年被無意間引入加拿大蒙特婁，此後迅速遍布整個北美洲，並擴散至澳洲與紐西蘭，現今除南極洲外各大洲皆有其蹤跡。牠的適應力極強，活躍於花園、公園、路邊、雜草叢生的田野、農場及城市地區。只要有十字花科或芸薹科植物的地方，幾乎都能見到牠的身影。

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed on plants in the mustard family (Brassicaceae). Host plants include cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, kale, Brussels sprouts, collards, radish, mustard, and horseradish — essentially any vegetable in the cabbage or mustard family. The caterpillars can be serious garden pests, sometimes eating plants down to bare stems. Their voracious appetite is remarkable: a caterpillar's body mass can more than double every single day during feeding!
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲以十字花科（Brassicaceae）植物為食，寄主植物包括高麗菜、青花菜、花椰菜、羽衣甘藍、球芽甘藍、芥藍、白蘿蔔、芥菜及辣根——幾乎所有高麗菜與芥菜科的蔬菜都是牠的食物。毛毛蟲對菜園危害不小，有時能將整株植物啃食得只剩莖部。牠們的食量驚人：在大量進食期間，毛毛蟲的體重每天可以增加超過一倍！
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adult butterflies feed on flower nectar from a wide variety of plants. They especially prefer flowers from the mustard family, but also visit dandelions, red clover, asters, and mints. Interestingly, they prefer purple, blue, and yellow flowers over red or white ones, and can even see ultraviolet light — which helps them locate flowers with UV nectar guides invisible to the human eye. Despite the caterpillar being a pest, the adult butterfly is a beneficial pollinator of crop plants.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以各種植物的花蜜為食，尤其偏愛十字花科的花卉，也會訪蒲公英、紅花苜蓿、紫苑及薄荷等。有趣的是，牠們偏好紫色、藍色和黃色的花朵，更能看見紫外線光——這讓牠們能夠察覺花朵上肉眼不可見的紫外線蜜導（花蜜引導標記）。雖然毛毛蟲是農業害蟲，但成蝶卻是農作物重要的授粉者。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
The Cabbage White undergoes complete metamorphosis: egg → caterpillar → chrysalis → adult.
  • Eggs: Females lay 300 to 400 eggs in their lifetime, one at a time, on the undersides of host plant leaves. Eggs are pale white, turning more yellow as they age, and hatch in about one week.
  • Caterpillars: The bright green "imported cabbageworm" grows through 5 instars (molts) over about 2 to 3 weeks. It has a faint yellow stripe along its back. Its green color is excellent camouflage among leaf vegetables.
  • Chrysalis: Grey, green, yellow, or brown — about 19–20 mm long — blending in with plant stems and debris. Butterflies that pupate in autumn overwinter in the chrysalis stage and emerge in spring.
  • Adults: Adult lifespan is about 3 to 6 weeks total, with roughly 3 weeks spent as an adult butterfly.
  • Broods in California: In Southern California's warm climate, there are 3 to 5 generations per year. It is typically the first butterfly to appear in spring and one of the last flying in autumn.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
菜粉蝶經歷完全變態：卵 → 幼蟲 → 蛹 → 成蝶。
  • 卵：雌蝶一生可產下 300 至 400 粒卵，一次一粒，產於寄主植物葉片背面。卵初為淡白色，隨時間逐漸變黃，約一週後孵化。
  • 幼蟲：俗稱「菜青蟲」的鮮綠色毛毛蟲，經歷五次蛻皮，約需 2 至 3 週成長。背部有一條淡黃色縱紋，綠色外觀在葉菜叢中是極佳的保護色。
  • 蛹：灰色、綠色、黃色或褐色，長約 19 至 20 公釐，與植物莖桿及枯葉融為一體。秋季化蛹的個體會以蛹的形態越冬，待春天羽化。
  • 成蝶：整體壽命約 3 至 6 週，其中約 3 週為成蝶階段。
  • 加州世代數：在南加州溫暖的氣候下，每年可繁殖 3 至 5 個世代。通常是春天最早出現、秋天最晚消失的蝴蝶之一。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Female Cabbage Whites mate only once in their lifetime, as early adults, usually around midday when temperatures are warmest. Males actively patrol open areas searching for females. After mating, the female's behavior changes dramatically — she immediately begins the task of searching for suitable host plants on which to lay her eggs. She uses both vision and chemical receptors on her feet (taste receptors!) to identify the right plants.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
雌性菜粉蝶一生只交配一次，通常在羽化後不久、氣溫最高的正午前後進行。雄蝶積極在開闊區域飛行尋找雌蝶。交配後，雌蝶的行為發生明顯轉變——立即開始尋找適合的寄主植物產卵。牠同時利用視覺及腳上的化學感受器（味覺感受器！）來辨識合適的植物。

🦋 Sandhill Skipper ／ 沙丘弄蝶

Polites sabuleti — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

🌾 About Skippers 關於弄蝶： Skippers are a distinct group — sometimes called the "third group" between butterflies and moths. They have stout, hairy bodies, hooked antennae tips, and fast, darting flight — quite different from the fluttery flight of true butterflies. The Sandhill Skipper is one of California's most common and widespread skippers, found in almost any grassy area.

弄蝶是一個獨特的群體——有時被稱為蝴蝶與蛾之間的「第三群體」。牠們體型粗壯多毛，觸角尖端帶鉤，飛行快速急促——與真正蝴蝶飄逸的飛行方式截然不同。沙丘弄蝶是加州最常見、分布最廣的弄蝶之一，幾乎在任何草地都能發現。

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Sandhill Skipper is a small butterfly with a wingspan of 7/8 to 1¼ inches (2.2–3.2 cm). Males have yellow-orange upper wings with dark toothed borders and a sinuous dark stigma (a scent patch) on the forewing — unique to males. Females have heavier dark markings and almost transparent light spots, with no stigma. The underside of the hindwing in both sexes is yellow-tan with yellow veins and dark chevron patterns at the outer margin — a useful field mark for identification. Like all skippers, they hold their wings at a characteristic angle when perching.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
沙丘弄蝶是小型蝴蝶，翼展 2.2 至 3.2 公分。雄蝶正面為黃橙色，帶鋸齒狀黑色邊框及前翅上彎曲的深色氣味鱗（stigma）——是雄蝶獨有的特徵。雌蝶黑色花紋較重，有近乎透明的淡色斑點，無氣味鱗。雌雄後翅背面均為黃褐色帶黃色翅脈及黑色人字形圖案——有助於辨識的野外特徵。和所有弄蝶一樣，停棲時翅膀以特有角度展開。

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
Found from southern British Columbia south through California and northern Arizona to Baja California, and east to Wyoming and Colorado. Also introduced to Hawaii. A habitat generalist — found in alkali grasslands, moist meadows, lawns, salt marshes, sand dunes, sagebrush flats, and alpine areas up to high elevations. In Southern California it is frequently found in suburban lawns, parks, and any area with Bermuda grass or saltgrass. Whittier Narrows' open grassy areas and salt marsh habitat are ideal!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
分布從英屬哥倫比亞南部向南穿越加州至下加利福尼亞半島，向東至懷俄明州及科羅拉多州。也已引入夏威夷。棲地廣泛——包括鹼性草地、濕潤草地、草坪、鹽沼、沙丘、鼠尾草草原及高山地帶。在南加州，常見於郊區草坪、公園及任何有百慕達草或鹽草之處。惠提爾納洛斯的開闊草地及鹽沼棲地是理想環境！

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed on grasses. The main host plants are saltgrass (Distichlis spicata) and Bermuda grass (Cynodon dactylon). The adoption of Bermuda grass as a host — a common introduced lawn grass — has helped this species thrive in urban and suburban areas of Southern California. Caterpillars feed on grass leaves and rest in shelters of tied leaves.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲以禾草為食，主要寄主植物為鹽草（Distichlis spicata）及百慕達草（Cynodon dactylon）。採用百慕達草——一種常見的引進草坪草——作為寄主，幫助這種蝴蝶在南加州的城市及郊區環境中繁盛生長。毛毛蟲以草葉為食，在捆綁葉片形成的巢穴中休息。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adults feed on flower nectar from a variety of small flowers in their grassy habitats. Males use special scent scales (the stigma on the forewing) to produce pheromones to attract females. They are fast, low fliers, spending much of the day perching in grassy areas.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以棲地中各種小花的花蜜為食。雄蝶利用前翅上的特殊氣味鱗（stigma）產生費洛蒙吸引雌蝶。牠們飛行快速而低矮，大部分時間在草地上停棲活動。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis.
  • Eggs: Laid singly on or near host grass plants; hatch in 1 to 2 weeks depending on temperature.
  • Caterpillars: Develop through 5 instars in 3 to 10 weeks. Some subspecies' chrysalids overwinter and hatch as adults in spring.
  • Adults: The California lowland subspecies (P. s. sabuleti) can have up to 5 generations per year, with flights from March through November! At high elevations: only 1 generation per year (June–August).
🇹🇼 繁體中文
完全變態。
  • 卵：單粒產在寄主草植物上或附近；依溫度不同，1 至 2 週孵化。
  • 幼蟲：經歷 5 個齡期，歷時 3 至 10 週。部分亞種以蛹越冬，春天羽化。
  • 成蝶：加州低地亞種（P. s. sabuleti）每年可繁殖多達 5個世代，飛行期從三月延續至十一月！在高海拔地區：每年只有 1 個世代（6–8 月）。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Males perch all day in low grassy areas, watching for females passing by — a behavior called "perching." They use chemical pheromones produced by the special scent scales (stigma) on their forewings to attract females. Females deposit eggs singly on the host plant or on nearby plants. Unlike many butterflies, skippers often perch with wings spread flat, orienting their bodies to absorb maximum warmth from the sun.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
雄蝶整天在低矮草地停棲守候雌蝶——稱為「守候（perching）」行為。牠們利用前翅氣味鱗（stigma）產生的化學費洛蒙吸引雌蝶。雌蝶將卵單粒產在寄主植物上或附近。與許多蝴蝶不同，弄蝶常平展翅膀停棲，身體朝向能吸收最多陽光熱量的方向。

🦋 Sleepy Duskywing ／ 睡暮翅弄蝶

Erynnis brizo — Bilingual Docent Guide 中英對照導覽手冊

🌳 Oak Woodland Specialist 橡樹林專家： The Sleepy Duskywing is one of the earliest flying butterflies of the year — appearing as early as January in California. It is a specialist of oak woodland and chaparral, completely dependent on oak trees for its larval stage. Despite its dark, understated appearance, it is a fascinating and ecologically important part of California's oak ecosystem.

睡暮翅弄蝶是全年最早飛翔的蝴蝶之一——在加州最早在一月就可以見到。牠是橡樹林與灌木叢林的專一性物種，幼蟲階段完全依賴橡樹。儘管外觀低調暗淡，牠卻是加州橡樹生態系統中一個令人著迷且生態上不可或缺的物種。

🎨 Appearance ／ 外觀特徵

🇺🇸 English
The Sleepy Duskywing has a wingspan of 1¼ to 1¾ inches (3.2–4.5 cm). The upper wing surface is dark brown with two wide grayish-brown bands across the forewing and — this is the key field mark — NO transparent spots (most similar species have transparent spots, making their absence the best way to identify this species). Hindwings are almost completely brown with lighter brown spots. Males have a costal fold on the forewing containing yellow scent scales; females have a patch of scent scales on the abdomen. The antennae clubs are distinctively blunt-tipped. Like all Duskywings, it holds its wings spread flat when perching — looking like it is taking a nap, which is where the name "Sleepy" comes from!
🇹🇼 繁體中文
睡暮翅弄蝶翼展 3.2 至 4.5 公分。翅膀正面為深褐色，前翅有兩條寬灰褐色帶，而且——這是關鍵的辨識特徵——無透明斑點（大多數相似物種都有透明斑點，因此其缺席是辨識本物種最好的方法）。後翅幾乎全褐色，帶有較淡的褐色斑點。雄蝶前翅前緣有含黃色氣味鱗的摺疊構造；雌蝶腹部有一塊氣味鱗。觸角棒末端特別鈍圓。和所有暮翅弄蝶一樣，停棲時翅膀平展——看起來就像在打盹，「睡」字正是由此而來！

🗺️ Range & Habitat ／ 分布範圍與棲息地

🇺🇸 English
In the west: from California east to Utah and Wyoming, south to Arizona, New Mexico, and Baja California. In the east: from Manitoba south through the eastern US to Florida and Texas. Habitat: oak and oak-pine scrub, chaparral, and barrens on well-drained sandy or rocky soils. This butterfly is very habitat-specific — wherever scrub oaks grow. In Southern California, look for it in chaparral hillsides and oak woodlands adjacent to Whittier Narrows.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
西部：從加州向東至猶他州和懷俄明州，向南至亞利桑那州、新墨西哥州及下加利福尼亞半島。東部：從馬尼托巴向南穿越美國東部至佛羅里達州及德克薩斯州。棲地：排水良好的砂質或岩石土壤上的橡樹及橡樹松林灌叢、灌木叢林及荒地。這種蝴蝶的棲地需求非常專一——凡有矮橡樹生長之處。在南加州，可在惠提爾納洛斯附近的灌木叢林山坡及橡樹林中尋找。

🌿 What They Eat ／ 食物來源

🇺🇸 English — Caterpillars
Caterpillars feed exclusively on oak species (Quercus) — primarily scrubby oaks such as leather oak, grey oak, and coast live oak. In California, leather oak (Quercus durata) and grey oak (Quercus turbinella) are confirmed host plants. Caterpillars are pale green with a darker dorsal stripe and construct silk-lined leaf rolls for shelter, overwintering as fully grown caterpillars inside these leaf shelters.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 毛毛蟲
毛毛蟲專門以橡樹屬（Quercus）植物為食——主要是矮生橡樹，如皮革橡（Quercus durata）、灰橡（Quercus turbinella）及海岸活橡。在加州，皮革橡與灰橡是確認的寄主植物。毛毛蟲為淡綠色帶深色背縱紋，以絲質內襯的捲葉巢穴為棲所，以完全成長的毛毛蟲形態在這些葉巢中越冬。
🇺🇸 English — Adults
Adults feed on nectar from early spring flowers, including wild azalea, blueberry, blackberry, dandelion, and cinquefoils. Like all Duskywings, they also "puddle" at mud or damp soil for additional minerals and moisture.
🇹🇼 繁體中文 — 成蝶
成蝶以早春花卉的花蜜為食，包括野生杜鵑、藍莓、黑莓、蒲公英及委陵菜等。和所有暮翅弄蝶一樣，也會在濕泥或濕土旁「吸水」攝取額外的礦物質與水分。

🔄 Life Cycle & Lifespan ／ 生命週期與壽命

🇺🇸 English
Complete metamorphosis. The Sleepy Duskywing is univoltine — only ONE generation per year throughout its entire range (this is unusual for a California butterfly!).
  • Eggs: Pale green, dome-shaped with 15 prominent vertical ribs, laid singly on young oak leaves or buds. Hatch in about 8 days.
  • Caterpillars: Stout, pale green with darker dorsal stripes, building silk-leaf roll shelters. Overwinter as fully grown caterpillars — resuming development in early spring.
  • Chrysalis: Formed inside leaf litter or soil; greenish initially. Adults emerge in late winter to early spring.
  • Adults: Flight period in California is January through May — one of the earliest butterflies of the year! Adults live only a few weeks.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
完全變態。睡暮翅弄蝶為單化性（univoltine）——整個分布範圍內每年只有一個世代（這在加州蝴蝶中相當罕見！）。
  • 卵：淡綠色，圓頂形，帶 15 條明顯垂直肋紋，單粒產在嫩橡樹葉或葉芽上。約 8 天孵化。
  • 幼蟲：粗壯，淡綠色帶深色背縱紋，以絲質捲葉巢為棲所。以完全成長的毛毛蟲越冬——早春再恢復發育。
  • 蛹：形成於落葉層或土壤中，初期呈綠色。成蝶在冬末至早春羽化。
  • 成蝶：加州飛行期為一月至五月——是全年最早出現的蝴蝶之一！成蝶壽命只有數週。

💛 Mating Behavior ／ 求偶與交配行為

🇺🇸 English
Males perch in clearings and on hilltops throughout the day to watch for females ("hilltopping"). When male density is high, they also patrol around host plants. Males have a costal fold on the forewing containing yellow scent scales used to produce pheromones. Females deposit single eggs exclusively on young oak leaves or leaf buds, showing strict selectivity in choosing egg-laying sites.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
雄蝶整天在林中空地及山丘頂端停棲守候雌蝶（「登頂」行為）。雄蝶密度高時，也會在寄主植物周圍巡飛。雄蝶前翅前緣有含黃色氣味鱗的摺疊構造，用於產生費洛蒙。雌蝶將卵單粒產在嫩橡樹葉或葉芽上，選擇產卵地點非常嚴格。

🌍 Conservation Status ／ 保育現況

🇺🇸 English
Rated G5 — Globally Secure. However, it is listed as Threatened in Connecticut and Special Concern in New Jersey. The main threats are loss of oak scrub/chaparral habitat through development, fire suppression (which leads to forest closure and loss of open habitat), and degradation of well-drained sandy or rocky soil habitats. Because it is univoltine, populations cannot recover quickly from local disturbances.
🇹🇼 繁體中文
全球保育等級 G5（全球穩定）。然而，在康涅狄格州被列為「受威脅（Threatened）」，在新澤西州被列為特別關注物種。主要威脅包括開發造成的橡樹灌叢/灌木叢林棲地喪失、火災管制（導致森林封閉及開放棲地喪失）及砂質或岩石土壤棲地退化。由於是單化性物種，族群遭受地區性干擾後恢復緩慢。

Fun Fact for Visitors ／ 給訪客的趣味小知識

"The Sleepy Duskywing gets its name because adults are often found resting with wings spread flat on leaves or bark in the sun — looking like they're taking a nap! The name also hints at its early-season appearance, before most other butterflies have 'woken up' for the year."

「睡暮翅弄蝶的名字源於成蝶常常在葉片或樹皮上平展翅膀曬太陽休息——看起來就像在打盹！這個名字也暗示牠出現在早春，比大多數其他蝴蝶在一年中『甦醒』更早。」
